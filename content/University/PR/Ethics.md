### 🎓 **Ethics in Public Relations (PR)**

PR professionals balance four key loyalties:

* 🏛️ **Public Interest**
* 🏢 **Employer’s Interests**
* 🧑‍⚖️ **PRSA Standards**
* ❤️ **Personal Values**

**🧠 Mnemonic: PEPP — “Pepper your decisions with ethics”**

* Public
* Employer
* Profession (standards)
* Personal

---

### 🏢 **In-House PR Department**

* Writes **speeches**, articles, and **copy**.
* Handles **training** (for sales teams, executives, lawyers).
* Manages **internal planning** (budgets, coordination with finance).
* Owns **social media strategy** and campaigns.
* Coordinates **media relations** and **events**.
* More **manageable** (in terms of budget and time).

🧠 **Mnemonic: W-T-P-S-M-E = "Wise Tigers Play Smart Media Events"**

---

### 📦 **Outsourced PR Firm**

* Has more **competencies** across different fields.
* Access to **advertising** and **graphic design** experts.
* Brings **experience** and broader **resources** (e.g., research tools).
* Can be more **expensive** or **less predictable** with budgets and deadlines.

🧠 **Mnemonic: CAGE — “Outsource when you need a CAGE match of skills”**

* Competencies
* Advertising
* Graphic Design
* Experience
